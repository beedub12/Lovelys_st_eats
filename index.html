Beautiful Cuisine

Lovely's Lao Street Eats is a family-owned and operated food truck bringing the flavors of Thailand and Laos to the streets of Columbus. Our truck serves dishes made from family recipes enjoyed throughout our homes. We use only the freshest ingredients to craft pad thai, papaya salad, and other classic Southeast Asian specialties served to you.
